How To Care For A Pet

Pets need some basic things in order to live a healthy life:

  1. Safe Shelter
  2. A Loving Family
  3. Food and Water
  4. Health Care

Many successful pet owners make sure that they provide their animal with many other things to create a good quality of life, such as:

  • Plenty of love and affection
    • Walks
    • Petting
    • Talking
    • Companionship
  • Toys and treats
  • A clean place to live and sleep
  • Regular vet check-ups and grooming
    • Baths
    • Brushing
  • Healthy food and fresh water daily
